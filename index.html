How TotalClean runs your operation

1. Turn SOPs into workflows

We convert your current checklists and tribal knowledge into step-by-step workflows that every tech can follow from their phone.

2. Capture quality in the field

Supervisors complete QC passes in the app, attach photos, and flag issues. The system builds a quality history for every building and route.

3. Manage by exception

Instead of chasing every job, you only see exceptions, missed tasks, or low scores — with clear next actions and ownership.
